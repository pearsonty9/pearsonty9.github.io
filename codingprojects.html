Livability

Goal: This program uses the google maps API in order to create a rating system of the how livable a selected point is. The rating system is based on the distance and amount of different green spaces and utlities around the selected location such as fire stations, hospitals, and parks.

Purpose: This project was my team's and I's submission to the 2020 KnightHacks Hack-a-thon. We took the combined backgrounds and knowledge base of each of our team members in order to com up with an idea that we could all contribute to. As one of our team members knows a lot about sustainability they came up with the idea of a rating system using google maps to determine what utilities are around a selected location.
